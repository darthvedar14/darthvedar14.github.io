We at KRIBHCO are committed to Safety, health and environment as an important part of our business operations by integrating SHE into all facets of the business. We are continuously taking appropriate steps to secure the health, safety and welfare of people at work, to protect the public generally against risks to health and safety arising out of the work situation. We believe that all accidents are preventable and avoidable. KRIBHCOâ€™s HSE Management System is fully complied with ISO 9001, IS0 14001 and ISO 45001 System.

Health and Safety:

KRIBHCO conducts its business in a manner that protects the health and safety of all employees and Contractors or third party affected by its activities. We operate our facilities to ensure all Health and Safety Legal requirements are fully complied and We are committed towards continual improvements in the fields of Health, Safety and environment.

Our Integrated HSE policy governs and illustrates all the activities to ensure a safe and healthy workplace as well as a clean environment for both the employees and the community. The HSE policy is our commitment to manufacturing of products in a safe and environment conscious manner.

We are Committed for:

To conduct our activities in the utmost diligent manner and achieve the highest level of excellence in the field of HSE.

To manufacture our Product in a safe and environment conscious manner and ensure safe & healthy working environment.

To adopt sound manufacturing practices to minimize pollutants, occupational health and safety risks.

To improve our health, safety and environmental performance continually and to comply with all statutes and regulations concerning Environment, Health and Safety.

To ensure Process Hazard are analysed before Plant Modification or Expansion and to adopt Best Technology available for ensuring the Process Safety.

To Implement required safety measures at all the stages of lifecycle of product or process.

Effective and continuously evolving systems for work permits, Incident Reporting and Investigation, work place safety, inspections and audits.

To educate and create awareness among Employees and contractors on Latest HSE Issues, Accident Prevention technique, Occupational Health and Safety Culture change.

Emergency Response System

Our entire Fertilizer Complex is Protected with Active and Passive Fire Protection System. Entire complex is surrounded with Fire Water Network, Hydrants, Fire Water Monitor, MCP, Water Sprinklers, Medium Velocity and High Velocity Water Spar System to protect our Equipment and Control Room. The entire complex is connected with Fire and Gas detection system, Critical Control Room provided with Inert Gas Flooding System (FM 200). The design and selection of our Fire Safety Equipment are as per relevant Indian Standard and International Standard (NFPA).
