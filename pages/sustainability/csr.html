CSR Strategy

Since the inception, Corporate Social Responsibility has been an intrinsic part of KRIBHCO’s DNA. Our CSR strategy includes a large number of initiatives focused on rural and agricultural transformations which have enhanced livelihoods of more than 5.81 lakh farmers and families across the nation. We maintain close and continuous relation with communities living around our units and provide support in terms of education, health, infrastructure, safety and sanitation. We design our activities to support inclusive growth and to improve the quality of life of individuals and communities we are associated with.

Measurable Social impact

Our goal is to make a measurable impact on people, society, and the planet. And we are well on the way. We’re expanding access to quality education nationwide. We contribute annually to social change agents who are addressing global challenges. And our efforts to make KRIBHCO environmentally sustainable help preserve the planet.
