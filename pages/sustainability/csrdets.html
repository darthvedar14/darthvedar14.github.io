Corporate Social Responsibility

Building ethical standards & Socially responsible business Practices

CSR Strategy

CSR activities like Human Health Campaigns, Animal Health Campaigns, Sanitation Campaigns, Rural Sports & Drinking Water Facilities etc. are being carried out which has benefited more than 151 lakh farmers and families across the nation. under CSR initiatives we adopt village cluster for an intensive approach in our mission to serve the farming community and villagers for improving their livelihood.

CSR Initiatives

Providing Drinking Water in nearby Villages of Hazira Plant:

The society is providing safe and pure drinking water, in the neighboring villages i.e., Kawas and Ichhapore. In addition, construction of check-dams was also sponsored in the districts of Dharampur and Olpad.

Educational Aid to rural schools:
With a view to promote education in rural and tribal areas, the society has been supporting educational institutes by way of construction of Class-rooms, Sanitation Blocks, providing furniture, books and book-shelves for Libraries, Laboratory instruments, distribution of Uniform and education kits consisting books and stationary items etc. The society has also supported for construction of village public library. To provide better educational facilities to the nearby villages & township residents, KRIBHCO is running schools in Gujarati Medium and English medium at KRIBHCO township.

Infrastructure Support
The society has been supporting the local administration in creation and maintenance of infrastructure. Construction of Village approach roads at Kawas, Kakrapara, Malgama and other villages were aided so that village connectivity can be maintained even during monsoon season. Bus-stand sheds were also constructed.

Sanitation campaign
Various camps are held in rural areas for awareness regarding sanitation to promote Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. KRIBHCO has been a high accomplisher in marketing of City Compost made from Urban Waste and contributing in the “Swachh Bharat Mission”. KRIBHCO initiated co-marketing of City Compost made from urban waste since December, 2009. In order to popularize Compost, KRIBHCO is constantly educating farmers for its application in addition to chemical fertilizers.

Environment Preservation
The society continually supports the programmes that sensitize Environment Protection and Enrichment by way of organizing Tree-Plantation campaigns and participating in Agriculture Fairs/Exhibitions etc. With a view to curb use of plastics to reduce Beat Plastic Pollution, KRIBHCO has also started an initiative to reduce the use of plastic in everyday lives of its employees so that the burden of plastic pollution in the environment can be reduced. KRIBHCO has always come forward to contribute to the environmental causes. Along the similar lines, “Green KRIBHCO, Clean KRIBHCO” campaign is continued, in which cleanliness drives is being organized regularly in order to create awareness and sensitivity towards cleanliness amongst the residents and in nearby villages.

Philanthropic Activities
KRIBHCO Karmachari Sahkari Dhiran & Purvata Society Limited, the employees’ credit society collects warm clothes & blankets from Plant township residents and distributes it to poor and needy villagers in the tribal district of Dang area on regular basis. KRIBHCO Prerna Club is conducting regular medical check-up camps for the labours and their families in nearby labour colony. Additionally, other social welfare programmes like distribution of food grains, blankets, bed sheets and other woollen clothes to the poor families in nearby villages. Along with it, earthen pots are also distributed by the club to the poor families. The Bal Bhavan School (Pre-Nursery) is being run by the Prerna Club for the children of poor labours in township.

Ashirwad Club -Senior Citizen Club
KRIBHCO also supports “Ashirwad” in its Township at Surat where Senior Citizens are provided facilities for meetings, prayers, reading Newspapers and religious books. Also, celebrations of Foundation Day, Annual excursion trips to nearby religious places are organized as a welfare measure for the senior citizens

Pandemic alleviation Initiatives
As the nation is fighting against this deadly COVID 19 virus, KRIBHCO extended its generous help by Distribution of Food Packets (grocery/ration packs), Sanitizer’s & Masks etc. KRIBHCO distributed food packets to labours residing in nearby villages, contractual and temporary workers, security personnel The efforts of Society in CSR front has not only enabled in enriching the socio-industrial culture but has earned the reputation of KRIBHCO being an organization supporting the social cause.
