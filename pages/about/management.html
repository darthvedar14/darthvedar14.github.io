Meet Mr. Rajan Chowdhry

Mr.Rajan Chowdhry is a Commerce Graduate from SRCC, Delhi University and also a Fellow member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. Shri Rajanchowdhry has been working with KRIBHCO since June 1984. Before his elevation as Managing Director he served the Finance Director also. He was on deputation with KRIBHCO Fertilizers Limited as Chief Finance Officer and with Indraprastha Power Generation Company Ltd (IPGCL) &Pragati Power Corporation Limited (PPCL), Government of Delhi as Director Finance.

Meet Mr.V.S. Sirohi

Mr..V.S.Sirohi is a Post graduate in Management from IIM Ahmedabad. He has worked in various capacities in the Marketing, Material Management and Business & Strategy Development functions at Multi location in Gujarat Narmada Valley Fertilizers & Chemicals Ltd.(GNFC). In his long career with GNFC, he has also spearheaded the new business development initiatives in India & abroad.

Meet Mr. M. R. Sharma

Shri M R Sharma has taken over as Operations Director and Head of KRIBHCO Hazira Fertilizer Plant on 29th October, 2018. He is working for the last more than thirty eight years in KRIBHCO. He is a BE (Chemical) from IIT, Roorkee and is the first Graduate Engineer Trainee who joined KRIBHCO and reached to this position. He was born on 7th February, 1960. He is also a Director of Board of Gujarat State Energy Generation Ltd. (GSEG), a joint venture between Gujarat State Petroleum Corporation Ltd (GSPC) and KRIBHCO. Shri Sharma was the Chairman of Hazira Notified Area.

He has worked in various capacities in KRIBHCO Hazira and its Corporate Office at Noida. Prior to this, he was holding the responsibilities of Executive Director and Head of KRIBHCO, Hazira Plant. He has also worked as Chief General Manager (Production). He is widely travelled in India and abroad

Shri Sharma has more than 38 years of experience in operations of giant fertilizer plants in India and Oman. Under his inspiring leadership, KRIBHCO has been achieving new heights in all the spheres of operations. He has been deputed to OMIFCO as Urea Production Manager for two years.

Meet Mr Sunder Singh Yadav

Mr Yadav is Director (HR) of KRIBHCO, a multi-state cooperative society, one of the largest fertiliser organisations in India since December 2019. Prior to this assignment, he has worked as ED(HR) KRIBHCO and Chief Executive Officer of a social development organisation, Garmin Vikas Trust (GVT) established by KRIBHCO.

Mr Yadav is B. Tech by Academics with degree in Electronics and Communications and belongs to Indian Engineering Services of 1989 UPSC batch and more importantly, he has done his Masters from prestigious London School of Economics in Public Management.

18 years of his total 27 years in Government of India were devoted to working at various senior positions in telecom sector. He has been instrumental in implementing few large scale projects in telecom sector including planning and implementation of mobile networks in Ghaziabad and Noida.

He has also worked on deputation as Director in National Disaster Management Authority, Govt. of India from 2009 to 2011. During this period, he was instrumental for implementation of a very large size World Bank Funded project for cyclone risk mitigation.

Mr Yadav has also worked as Director in Election Commission of India from 2012 to 2014 for preparing the election machinery for General Elections 2014. During his tenure with ECI, he was credited for his key role in setting up India International Institute for Democracy and Election Management.
