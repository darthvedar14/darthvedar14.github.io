Rajan Chowdhry

Rajan Chowdhry is a Commerce Graduate from SRCC, Delhi University and also a Fellow member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. Shri Rajanchowdhry has been working with KRIBHCO since June 1984. Before his elevation as Managing Director he served the Finance Director also. He was on deputation with KRIBHCO Fertilizers Limited as Chief Finance Officer and with Indraprastha Power Generation Company Ltd (IPGCL) &Pragati Power Corporation Limited (PPCL), Government of Delhi as Director Finance.

V.S. Sirohi

V.S.Sirohi is a Post graduate in Management from IIM Ahmedabad. He has worked in various capacities in the Marketing, Material Management and Business & Strategy Development functions at Multi location in Gujarat Narmada Valley Fertilizers & Chemicals Ltd.(GNFC). In his long career with GNFC, he has also spearheaded the new business development initiatives in India & abroad.

M. R. Sharma

M R Sharma is a B.E. (Chemical) from IIT, Roorkee. He has worked in various capacities in KRIBHCO He was holding the responsibilities of Executive Director of KRIBHCO, Hazira Plant, Production Head as Chief General Manager (Production). Shri Sharma has more than 38 years of experience in operations of giant fertilizer plants in India and Oman.

Sunder Singh Yadav

S.S. Yadav is B.Tech(Electronics and Communications) and IES 1989. He has done his Masters from London School of Economics in Public Management. He has also worked on deputation as Director in National Disaster Management Authority, Govt. of India from 2009 to 2011. Mr Yadav has also worked as Director in Election Commission of India from 2012 to 2014. Prior to this assignment, he has worked as ED(HR) KRIBHCO and Chief Executive Officer Gramin Vikas Trust (GVT).

R. S. Mathur

A Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India with experience of more than three decades in the field of Accounting, Audit, Taxation, Treasury etc. He has worked in finance functions of all areas viz. Plant, Marketing, Internal Audit and Corporate Finance and has held various senior positions in KRIBHCO, KFL and OMIFCO

VSR Prasad

VSR Prasad has done his Masters in Business Administration with specialization in Marketing. At present he is holding the portfolio of Zonal In- charge South- West Zone, Cooperative Relations and Port Operations.

During his 32 years of service in KRIBHCO he has worked in various capacities like Area Manager, Chief State Manager, Chief General Manager & Zonal In-charge for East, South- West Zones.

Prior to his joining at KRIBHCO Mr. Prasad has worked in IFFCO.
