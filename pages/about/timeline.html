1980

Incorporation

Krishak Bharati Cooperative Limited (KRIBHCO) was incorporated on 17th April 1980 as a national level Multi State Cooperative Society to implement first gas based “state-of-the-art” high capacity Fertilizer Complex at Hazira District-Surat, Gujarat, India based on natural gas from Bombay High/ South Bassein.

1982

Laying of Foundation Stone

- KRIBHCO Hazira Plant's Foundation Stone was laid by Late Smt. Indira Gandhi on 5th Feb 1982

- Seeding Program

1986

Commercial Production

On 1st March,1986 KRIBHCO's Commercial Production Begins

1991

Certified Seeds

Seed Multiplication Program for Certified Seeds. Keeping in view importance of the Quality Seeds in enhancing the agricultural production, KRIBHCO initiated Seed Multiplication Programme to provide quality certified seeds of the crops.

1992

GVT

Gramin Vikas Trust (GVT) started as a division of KRIBHCO.

1995

Bio-Fertilizer

- Bio-Fertilizer Plant at Hazira Commissioned for production of Semi Solid Bio-Fertilizer
- Joint Venture for OMIFCO with Oman Oil and IFFCO.

2003

OMIFCO

OMIFCO Foundation stone laid.(JV of KRIBHCO, OMAN OIL & IFFCO)

2005

KSFL

- JV of KRIBHCO & Shyam group for KRIBHCO Shyam Fertilizer Limited(KSFL)
- OMIFCO Commercial Production Begins. KRIBHCO’s Joint Venture project with Oman Oil Company came on stream in Sultanate of Oman.

2009

KRIL

KRIBHCO incorporated Kribhco Infrastructure Limited(KRIL), a 100% subsidiary to venture into logistics business

Read More

2010

City Compost

- Marketing of high yield Hybrid seeds of various crops like Bt Cotton
- City compost - An organic manure made product without any side-effects was introduced by KRIBHCO. It can be used in all kinds of crops. It is used to improve soil health & physical properties of soil and may obtain desired quality produce.

2011

Liquid Bio-Fertilizers

Liquid Bio Fertilizers, Single Super Phosphate and Complex Fertilizers added to product basket

Read More

2012

Revamp

The Hazira Fertilizer Complex was subsequently revamped to increase its production capacity by 4.65 LMTPA of Urea. After the revamp, the rated capacity has been enhanced to 4 x 1662.5 MTPD Urea (21.95 LMTPA) and 2 x 1890 MTPD Ammonia (12.47 LMTPA). With this, the Hazira fertilizer complex has became largest single location urea plant in India at that time.



2013

Neem Coated Urea

Neem Coated Urea production and packaging started.

Neem Coated Urea production and packaging started.

2015

Promotional Program for Zinc Sulphate

KRIBHCO started promotional program for providing quality Zinc sulphate as Zinc sulphate mono-hydrate (33%) and Zinc sulphate heptahydrate(21%) to farmers. This helps overcome "Khaira" disease in paddy.

2016

KSFL becomes 100% subsidiary of KRIBHCO and becomes KFL

KFL owns a gas based Fertilizer Complex at Shahjahanpur, U.P. strategically located in North India, in high urea consumption belt. The fertilizer complex consists of 1520 MPTD Ammonia Plant (annual capacity of 5.02 Lakh MT) and 2620 MTPD Urea Plant (annual capacity of 8.64 Lakh MT).
