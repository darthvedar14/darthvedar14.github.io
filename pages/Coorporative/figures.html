INDIAN COOPERATIVE MOVEMENT AT A GLANCE

The Government of India, immediately after independence, realising the significant role cooperatives can play in the development of national economy, recognised them as a third economic sector. They were charged with the responsibility for taking care the needs and aspirations of rural India with special emphasis on uplifting the small and marginal farmers as well as weaker -sections of the society.
