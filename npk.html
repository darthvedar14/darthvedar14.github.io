Granular NPK complexes are free flowing and do not pose any problem during handling and storage. However, exposure of material for long period to very high humidity may cause caking. Therefore, NPK complexes are bagged in quality tested HDPE bags to prevent ingress of moisture.

NPK (10:26:26) is a complex fertilizer which contains all three major nutrients 10% Nitrogen, 26 % Phosphorous and 26 % Potash which are easily and quickly available to plants.

  • Contain Phosphorous and Potassium in 1:1 ratio.
  • Available in 50 Kg. packing.
  • Ideal complex fertilizer for crops which require high P during the initial stage of growth.
  • A quality source of usable N P & K. for all crops.
  • Helpful for quick development & growth of plant roots.
  • Helpful for vigorous growth of crop with quick germination
  • Useful to increase tillering and healthy plant growth.
  • Helpful for seed, fruit development and grain filling.
  • Helpful for good stability for entire maturity period of crop.
  • Required less time and labor to apply as a single fertilizer having all nutrients & makes the cost of input cheaper to farmers

Used in a basal application in crops like Wheat, Paddy, Maize, Pulses, Sugarcane, Vegetables, Cotton etc. Application is recommended in split doses for better use efficiency.

NPS (20:20:0:13) is a complex fertilizer which contains two major nutrients 20% Nitrogen, 20% Phosphorous and 13 % Sulphur which are easily and quickly available to plants.

  • Contain Nitrogen and Phosphrous in 1:1 ratio.
  • Available in 50 Kg. packing.
  • Ideal complex fertiliser for Oilseeds and Pulses crops.
  • Helpful for sustaining the protein and oil quality in crop seed.
  • Helpful for good stability for entire maturity period of crop.
  • Required less time and labor to apply as a single fertilizer having all nutrients & makes the cost of input cheaper to farmers.

Suitable for all crops both for initial application and top dressing. Can be used in cereal, pulses, oilseed etc.
