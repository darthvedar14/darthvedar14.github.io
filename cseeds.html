CERTIFIED SEEDS

Seed multiplication programme was initiated by KRIBHCO during 1990-91 to provide quality seeds to farmers with the motto “Either Certified Seed or No Seed”. First unit was established at Aligarh (UP) with the capacity of 20,000 qtl./ year. Presently KRIBHCO produces the certified seeds of cereals , pulses , oil seed, spices and fodder crops at it‟ s modern 15 seed processing units located in 7 states. The certified seeds are annually produced by progressive farmers according to standards & prescribed seed production practices. The seeds produced are being made available to the farmers through Krishak Bharati Sewa Kendra, cooperative societies and State cooperative marketing federations in different states.

As per minimum seed certification standards there are certain standards to be meet out before it has been certified:

  • Varietal Purity: The first & foremost requirement is that the seed must be of an improved variety released by either the central or a state university release committee for general cultivation and notified by the Ministry of Agriculture, Govt. of India.
  • Genetic Purity: Means true to the type to it‟s parents genetically and free from other varieties seeds of the same crop species. No mixture of other variety or other crop seed. Amount of contamination permitted range 0 - 0.1% but contamination by seeds of other varieties of same crop is generally not permitted.
  • Physical Purity:Means free from inert matter ( sand , pebbles ,soil particle ,straw etc.) And defective seeds (broken ,insect infected ,shriveled ,unfit for germination) in case of maize permissible contamination is 1.0 %.
  • Germination: a high percentage of germination is required to obtain a good crop. Germination standard of seed certification varies from one crop to another like Maize(90%), Wheat(85%), Paddy(80%), Jowar(80%), gram(85%), Arhar(75%) mustard(85%)etc.
  • Free from weed seeds: must be free from weed seeds to prevent spreading weeds through seeds & reduces losses caused by weeds.
  • Free from diseases: must be free from seed-born diseases . Presence of seed-born pathogens prevented by either seed treatment or effective control of diseases in standing crop./li>
  • Moisture content : certified seed must be dried to an optimum moisture level for effective processing, seed viability & safe storage . Optimum moisture level varies from 7% in case of oil seeds & pulses to 12% in case of cereals in bags packing. Where as the moisture 1-2% lower in containers made of metals.
